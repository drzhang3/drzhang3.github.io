About me

I’m currently a master student of Beijing Institute of Technology(BIT), majoring Intelligent Information Processing and Control advised by Prof.Yuanqing Xia. I’ll graduate with a Master’s degree from School of Automation, BIT, in July 2021. My research interests lie within deep learning for Graph Neural network and Natural Language Processing. I am also involved in Machine Learning problems like optimization algorithms. I was a research intern of Simple Log Serive(SLS) Team in Ali Cloud, advised by Guiyang Liu. I’ll be a research intern of Wechat in Tecent during 2020 summer.
